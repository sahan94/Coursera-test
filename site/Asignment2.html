Crispy Chicken

Crispy chiken is very delicious food which can make ourself easily. So don't go outside, here is the magic for the healthy, Homemade Crispy chicken.🍗

Ingredients 📋

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons garlic salt 🧂
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 3 teaspoons pepper 🌶️
  • 2-1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 2 large eggs 🥚
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt 🧂
  • 2 broiler/fryer chickens (3-1/2 to 4 pounds each), cut up 🍖
  • Oil for deep-fat frying

Preparation 🍳

  1. Flour mixture preparation - Combine following:
    1. 2-2/3 cups flour
    2. 2 tablespoons garlic salt
    3. 1 tablespoon paprika
    4. 2-1/2 teaspoons pepper
    5. 2-1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  2. Egg mixture preparation 🥣
    1. Beat Eggs & 1-1/2 cup water
    2. 1 teaspoons salt
    3. 1-1/3 cups flour
    4. 1/2 teaspoons pepper
  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture
  4. Place that chiken in flour mixture
  5. Fry chicken in a deep fat fryer 🔥
